School districts across eastern Idaho announce closures amid coronavirus concerns

By Rett Nelson, EastIdahoNews.com

American Heritage Charter School: From March 13-19, we will have Virtual/Online school for all students.
Spring Break will begin as normal March 20. Kids are scheduled to return to class on March 30. Bear Lake School District 33: Due to the coronavirus concerns, Bear Lake School District schools will be closed for three weeks. They are urging families to self-quarantine as much as possible to stop the spread. Bingham Academy Charter High School: After extensive meetings Monday and watching new developments from our area, around the state, and the national stage, Bingham Academy will close through spring break Sunday, March 29. This includes all extracurricular activities. As that date approaches, we will announce plans for the week beginning on Monday, March 30 and beyond. Blackfoot School District 55: School has been canceled March 17 through April 3. The board decided to begin spring break immediately.
We will hold our regularly scheduled spring break March 23-27. The board also approved an extended for the week after, with the consideration of school reconvening on April 6.
